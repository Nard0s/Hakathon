Techtonich Tribe

Debre Berhan University (DBU): Techtonic Tribe operates within the context of Debre Berhan University, which was established in 2007 as a public higher education institution. DBU has been diligently working toward its threefold mission: Producing Efficient Graduates: DBU offers research-assisted quality education to equip students with the skills needed for the workforce. Problem-Solving Research: The university engages in research on selected thematic areas to address real-life challenges. Community Engagement: DBU provides training, consultancy services, technology transfer, and innovations to benefit the community1

Vision

DBU’s Vision for a Thriving Tech Community At Dallas Baptist University (DBU), our tech community is more than just a gathering of students and professionals—it’s a vibrant ecosystem that fosters innovation, collaboration, and lifelong learning. Let’s delve into the details: Empowering Innovators and Collaborators: Continuous Learning and Exploration: We envision a space where curious minds thrive. Regular workshops, coding bootcamps, and tech talks expose students to the latest trends and emerging technologies. Networking and Industry Connections: Our community isn’t confined to campus boundaries. It extends to industry leaders, startups, and alumni. Imagine tech meetups where students engage in meaningful conversations with professionals, exchange ideas, and even find internship opportunities. Diversity and Inclusion: We celebrate diversity in all its forms—gender, ethnicity, backgrounds, and perspectives. Our tech community thrives when everyone feels welcome. Inclusion isn’t just a buzzword; it’s our ethos. Impactful Solutions for Real-World Challenges: Problem Solvers Unite: DBU’s tech community isn’t content with theoretical knowledge. We roll up our sleeves and tackle real-world problems. Imagine students collaborating on projects that address climate change, healthcare accessibility, or urban mobility. Our hackathons aren’t just about winning prizes; they’re about making a positive impact. Entrepreneurial Spirit: Startups are born here. Our community encourages students to think like entrepreneurs. From ideation to execution, DBU provides mentorship, resources, and a supportive environment. Who knows? The next disruptive tech company might emerge from our labs. Ethical Tech Leaders: We don’t just code; we consider the ethical implications of our work. Privacy, security, and responsible AI are integral parts of our discussions. DBU’s tech graduates are not only skilled but also conscious of their societal impact. Join the movement. DBU’s tech community isn’t just a vision—it’s a movement. It’s the late-night coding sessions, the “aha” moments during lectures, and the friendships forged over shared lines of code. It’s where faith and technology converge, creating a brighter future. Welcome to DBU’s tech family. Let’s innovate fearlessly, serve selflessly, and leave a legacy that transcends ones and zeros.
